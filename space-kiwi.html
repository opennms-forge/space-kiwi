Space Kiwi

A long time ago, in a galaxy far,
far away....

It is a period of critical outages. Kiwi spaceships flying through the galaxy, searching for the things to fix broken by the evil Major Fault.

During the voyage, Kiwi engineers managed to code a software powerful enough to stop Major Fault's evil plans to disconnect the galaxy.

Pursued by the Major's sinister agents, the Space Kiwi Ulf - awarded with the Monitoring Service Medal - races home aboard his starship, custodian of the mighty software OpenNMS that can save his people and restore network connectivity for the galaxy....
